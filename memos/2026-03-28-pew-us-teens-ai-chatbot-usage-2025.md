# Pew U.S. Teens AI Chatbot Usage (2025)

Context: Pew Research Center, survey of U.S. teens ages 13-17 conducted Sept. 25-Oct. 9, 2025 (`Teens, Social Media and AI Chatbots 2025`).

## Overall Usage (% of U.S. teens ages 13-17 who say they ever use each AI chatbot)

| Chatbot | Usage |
|---|---:|
| ChatGPT | 59% |
| Gemini | 23% |
| Meta AI | 20% |
| Copilot | 14% |
| Character.ai | 9% |
| Claude | 3% |

## Usage by Race/Ethnicity (% who say they ever use each AI chatbot)

| Chatbot | White teens | Hispanic teens | Black teens | U.S. teens |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| ChatGPT | 55% | 59% | 65% | 59% |
| Gemini | 17% | 26% | 32% | 23% |
| Meta AI | 15% | 23% | 32% | 20% |
| Copilot | 13% | 13% | 19% | 14% |
| Character.ai | 6% | 10% | 16% | 9% |
| Claude | 2% | 4% | 4% | 3% |

Notes:
- White and Black teens include respondents reporting only one race and not Hispanic.
- Hispanic teens can be of any race.
