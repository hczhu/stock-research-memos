Once that problem is important, systematic, widespread, and needs to be done over and over again,  in the same way, then you will institutionalise it. The institutionalisation might be a 5-megabyte Excel spreadsheet, or a database. But there's a big, fuzzy space around the edges where you want to ask a question that you haven't asked before, maybe your existing system of record can do that, and maybe it can't. LLMs will be a very fertile space, for extending those kinds of improvisatory questions inside existing software, and as a new substrate sitting next to Excel.
