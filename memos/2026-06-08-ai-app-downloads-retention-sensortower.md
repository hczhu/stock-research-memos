- ## Investment implications
	- **OpenAI / ChatGPT**: The screenshot supports the view that ChatGPT remains the consumer AI product-quality leader. Retention is the strongest evidence because download growth can be bought or bundled, but Day 30 usage is harder to fake.
	- **Anthropic / Claude**: Claude's **+1.1K%** download change is strategically important because Anthropic has historically been stronger in developer/enterprise workflows than consumer mobile distribution. The weak point is retention: **8.41% Day 30** still looks far below ChatGPT.
	- **Google / Gemini**: Gemini likely benefits from massive distribution, but the visible **-24.7%** download change and **8.64% Day 30 retention** suggest weaker consumer pull than ChatGPT. This is a product-risk signal for Google's AI consumer surface, even if Gemini remains strategically strong inside search, Android, Workspace, and API usage.
	- **Meta AI**: Meta AI download growth (**+87.2%**) plus a late-period move toward ~5M weekly downloads is notable, but **4.51% Day 30 retention** suggests usage may be less durable as a standalone app. The stronger Meta AI thesis may still be embedded distribution across WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and Ray-Ban, not standalone app retention.
	- **xAI / Grok**: Grok appears to have had a strong early-January download spike and the second-best Day 30 retention (**12.26%**), but weekly downloads faded toward ~1.8M by early June. The product may be stickier for a niche audience than its current download trend implies.
