- ## China AI Inference — Token Consumption & Pricing Projections
	- **Source**: Field notes from conversations with domestic Chinese AI/cloud vendors, April 2026
	- **Thesis**: Token consumption in China is on a 10x annual growth trajectory; inference unit economics are approaching breakeven in 2026, with full breakeven (including training) possible in 2027H2–2028

	| Milestone | Timing | Condition |

- ## Key Observations
	- The 10x annual growth prediction made in Aug–Sep 2025 was widely considered too aggressive at the time; current field evidence suggests it may still be *conservative*
	- Revenue math: 500 trillion tokens/month at RMB 100 per 100M tokens = ~RMB 500M/month in gross inference revenue
	- Inference profitability is structurally achievable in 2026; the drag is model training capex, which pushes full breakeven to 2027–2028
	- Strategic investment in AI infrastructure in China likely continues for 1–2 more years before full unit economics turn positive

- ## Investment Implications
	- Strong demand signal for inference compute (GPU, networking, memory) in China through 2026–2027
	- ByteDance's 10–15x revenue growth projection implies rapid monetization of model APIs domestically
	- Inference margin improvement is on a clear path; training cost amortization is the remaining overhang
	- 10x token growth per year, if sustained, is a structural tailwind for the entire AI infrastructure supply chain
