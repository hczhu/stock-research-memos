- ## Context & Investment Implications
	- Supreme Electronics is a direct real-time demand signal for Samsung memory into Taiwan — a hub for AI server ODMs (Foxconn, Quanta, Wistron) who build hyperscaler infrastructure
	- +191% YoY in March and +137% for Q1 are not price-only effects — volume is also surging, consistent with AI server build-out accelerating into H1 2026
	- The +70.7% MoM jump from February to March suggests demand is front-loaded and accelerating within the quarter, not plateauing
	- Corroborates the DRAM pricing supercycle thesis: both price and volume are moving up simultaneously, which is the strongest possible revenue environment for memory makers
	- Taiwan ODM demand feeding into US hyperscaler capex (Microsoft, Google, Meta, Amazon) — this data point closes the loop from hyperscaler capex commitments to actual chip pull-through
