## SAP pushes AI to its customers with FDE
- ### Joule, SAP's AI copilot
- AI copilot Joule debuted in September 2023, integrated with SAP’s human resources software SucessFactors. The chatbot set out to help users find information faster and “to surface smarter insights,” the company said at the time. Since then, SAP has rolled out Joule assistants for different solutions, including one for developers to build SAP applications quicker and one for consultants to speed up cloud transformation projects.
-
- Two major implementation partners that sell the software in the US and Germany, SAP’s biggest markets, said they don’t see demand for the AI solutions in their talks with customers, and some clients said the new products aren’t yet worth the expense.
- Volkswagen AG, a large SAP customer, tested Joule and did not find it mature enough, a person familiar with the matter said. The company didn’t see how the tool would save substantial money or resources, the person added.
