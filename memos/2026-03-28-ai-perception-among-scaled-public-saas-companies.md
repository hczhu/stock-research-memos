## AI Perception Among Scaled Public SaaS Companies

The survey measures the **net percentage of respondents who say AI "helps" each vendor**.

### Net % Saying AI Helps

| Vendor | Net % AI Helps |
|---|---:|
| Salesforce | +40% |
| ServiceNow | +32% |
| Snowflake | +30% |
| Cloudflare | +29% |
| Databricks | +24% |
| Datadog | +14% |
| GitLab | +11% |
| Atlassian | +9% |
| Workday | +5% |
| MongoDB | +3% |
| Figma | −22% |
| Confluent | −29% |

### Observations

- Clear AI perception leaders: Salesforce, ServiceNow, Snowflake, Cloudflare.
- Data and infrastructure platforms skew strongly positive.
- Application-layer and design tools show weaker or negative sentiment.
- Confluent and Figma are notable negative outliers.
