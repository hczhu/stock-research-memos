- ## Directions
	- Live events (focus on sports)
	- Everyday product: Roblox used increasingly spontaneously; headroom to become a daily-use product
	- Real-world shopping
	- Social communication

---

- ## Monetization
	- Push of long-range technology in H2 2023 (facial animation, voice, avatar interactivity) may have introduced drag on the platform
	- Launched **video ads product**; enhanced self-serve ad manager; third-party integrations with IAS and PubMatic
	- Testing **real-world shopping** in Roblox with partners Walmart and e.l.f.
	- Cost to serve: **$12.75 per 1,000 hours** (Q2 2023) → **$9.61** (Q2 2024) — **25% reduction**
	- Infrastructure, trust & safety as % of bookings: **17% → 13%**

---

- ## Users and Engagement
	- Key areas to reverse engagement downtrend:
		1. Live ops — brought back platform-wide events in March (kicked off with Hunt; then The Classic + The Games)
		2. Ecosystem health
		3. Economy improvements
		4. Raw performance and quality
	- **Dress To Impress** emerged organically — doubled DAUs from Q1 to Q2
