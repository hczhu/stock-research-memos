- **Source**: Roblox 2025 Q1 earnings call and filings
- **Theme**: Monetization leverage accelerating — infra and R&D as % of revenue falling sharply; multi-stream revenue model taking shape

---

- ## Directions
	- North Star: capture **10% of the $180B global gaming content market** flowing through Roblox
	- Search and Discovery: spend now more broadly distributed
		- Ranked 11–50 experiences accounted for **40% of spending growth** vs. only 5% in Q1 last year
		- Genre expansion accelerating: RPG, sports & racing, shooters
	- Live beta of text generation API using an LLM; creators can use directly in-experience to power conversational AI characters
	- Over **300 Roblox-created ML models** running in the system

---

- ## Revenue Streams
	- Three streams: (1) Northstar — 10% of $180B global gaming market; (2) Ads; (3) E-commerce

---

- ## Monetization / Cost Structure
	- Infrastructure and Trust & Safety: **23% of revenue** (down from 28% in 2024Q1)
		- Increase offset by AI-driven moderation efficiency and infra optimization
	- R&D: **36% of revenue** (down from 45% in 2024Q1)
	- **Differential Robux Pricing** avoids payment processing fees (Apple tax); drove up developer exchange fees as a % of total bookings
	- **Price optimization tool**: helps creators A/B test prices for game passes and developer products; median earnings increase of **4%** for eligible developers using the tool
	- **Regional Pricing Recommendations**: early testing with top experiences showed promising results
	- **100+ Roblox developers** earned over $1M in the last 12 months
	- **Google Ads integration**: Rewarded Video ads available through Google Ad Manager, enabling brands to reach Roblox's user base programmatically
