- ## Reporting / Geography
	- Xbox user geographic location is not collected — Xbox users are grouped into Rest of World DAUs for reporting purposes

---

- ## Monetization Model Mechanics
	- Bookings = virtual currency (Robux) sales reflected as one-time purchases or monthly subscriptions via payment processors or prepaid cards
	- Cost of revenue = primarily third-party payment processing fees charged by distribution channels in connection with Robux sales
	- Context: Payment processing was $317.1M, or **26% of bookings**, in the 9 months ended September 30, 2020 (included as baseline for processing cost trajectory)
