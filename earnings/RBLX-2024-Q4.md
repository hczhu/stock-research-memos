---

- ## Directions
	- Genre expansion: 2025 focus on RPGs, sports and racing, and action
	- Live Ops: Reintroduced platform-wide events in Q1 2024
	- AI: Open-sourced voice safety model on Hugging Face — downloaded over **20,000 times**

---

- ## Monetization
	- Pushing Battle Royale on low-end mobile; engineering to support BR on **2GB RAM Android** devices in difficult networking markets like India with no performance compromise

---

- ## Users and Engagement
	- December uptime hit **five nines (99.999%)**
	- **5 of the 10 top-grossing 2024 movies** had activations on Roblox
	- DAU growth stepped down from Q3 to Q4; management cited three factors:
		- Lapped PlayStation release in Q4
		- Lapped new Xbox user sign-ups from 2023Q4
		- Turkey ban took effect August 2024 (economic impact was less than the user count impact)
