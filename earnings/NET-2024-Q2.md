- **Source**: Cloudflare Q2 2024 earnings materials cited in `Cloudflare $NET - Investment thesis.pdf`
- **Theme**: Developer credibility keeps strengthening while the company continues to move upmarket

---

- ## Products and Developer Platform
	- **Cloudflare Workers** reached **2.4M active developers**, up from **2.0M** the prior quarter
	- Workers ranked as the **4th most popular cloud platform** after the hyperscalers in the 2024 Stack Overflow developer survey
	- The source deck argues Cloudflare is **more admired by developers** than the hyperscalers

---

- ## Customers
	- A **leading Australian technology company** that began as a free customer in 2016 signed a **2-year, $17.5M** contract
		- **$7.2M** of the deal was expansion
		- Product scope now spans remote app access, Workers, serverless development, and bot management
	- A **Fortune 500 gaming company** signed a **2-year, $3M** contract for advanced app security, performance, and **R2 storage**
		- They migrated from two incumbent vendors to Cloudflare
		- Reasons cited: completeness of solution, better network performance and scale, and a unified control plane

---

- ## Read-Through
	- Cloudflare’s move upmarket is visible in both customer quality and product breadth
	- The developer love matters because it gives the company a distribution channel that most network-security vendors do not have
