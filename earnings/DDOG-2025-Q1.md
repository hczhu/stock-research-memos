- **Source**: Datadog Q1 2025 earnings call
- **Theme**: AI-native cohort at 8.5% of ARR; security at 7,500+ customers; Flex Logs and Database Monitoring hitting $50M ARR; acquisitions of Eppo and Metaplane

---

- ## Products
	- **New products**
		- Flex Logs exceeds **$50M in ARR**
		- Database Monitoring approaching $50M ARR, growing **60% YoY**, adopted by 5,000+ customers
	- **AI**
		- 4,000+ customers used Datadog AI integrations; number doubled YoY
	- **Security — DevSecOps**
		- Cloud security identifies vulnerabilities in hosts, containers, Kubernetes clusters, and IaaC
		- Code security identifies vulnerabilities in code from development to production — adopted by 1,000+ customers
		- **7,500+ customers** with security products (~1/4 of total customer base)
		- Over half of Fortune 500 customers use security products
	- **Data monitoring**
		- Data Streams Monitoring for streaming data (Kafka)
		- Data Jobs Monitoring for Spark and large data transformation jobs
	- R&D investment takes **2–3 years to realize in revenue** due to product build timelines

- ## Customers
	- **11 $10M+ deals** in the quarter
	- Dollar bookings for new logos up **over 70% YoY**
	- 7-figure annualized deal with one of the largest U.S. car manufacturers
	- 7-figure annualized deal with a major Latin American bank replacing 3 existing tools
	- 7-figure annualized deal with a major American pet supplies company with $1M annual savings
	- Re-signed an insurtech customer with a 6-figure annualized deal (had left to build own OSS solution, then returned)
	- **AI-native cohort**
		- Accounts for **8.5% of Q1 ARR** vs. 6% prior quarter and 3.5% a year prior
		- Contributed **6 percentage points of YoY growth**
		- 10+ customers in the cohort spending $1M+ annually
		- Management caution: "We've seen that movie before. Cloud-native customers grew very fast out of COVID then had to optimize sharply."

- ## Acquisitions
	- **Eppo** (founded 2021) — feature management and experimentation platform
	- **Metaplane** (founded 2019) — data observability platform; helps prevent, detect, and resolve data availability and quality issues
