- **Source**: Datadog Q3 2025 earnings call
- **Theme**: 26% revenue beat on AI acceleration; OpenAI 9-figure renewal; security ARR accelerating; Toto model on HuggingFace

---

- ## Products
	- Digital experience products together exceed **$300M in ARR**; Product Analytics adopted by 1,000+ customers
	- **Security ARR growth 55% YoY** in Q3, up from 45% last quarter
	- **AI use cases**
		- Number of LLM spans customers send to Datadog has more than **quadrupled**
		- Toto OpenWave time series forecasting model downloaded **almost half a million times on HuggingFace**

- ## Customers
	- 7-figure annualized deal with a leading European telco — **largest ever land deal in Europe**. Previous setup was expensive, inefficient, and wasn't scaling.
	- 7-figure annualized deal with a leading financial risk and analytics company. Fragmented tooling led to major incidents taking multiple days and hundreds of engineers to resolve.
	- 7-figure annualized expansion with a Fortune 500 financial services company. One business unit was manually hosting and maintaining **93 separate instances of open-source tooling**.
	- 7-figure annualized expansion with a Fortune 500 heavy equipment company — replacing open-source log solution with Datadog Log Management and Flex Logs.
	- **9-figure annualized expansion with a leading AI company** (OpenAI). Long-time Datadog customer; expanded usage of multiple products; secured better economics for a higher commitment with an early renewal. (Per press reports, Datadog offered a discount to retain.)

- ## Monetization
	- **26% revenue growth beat the 19% expectation** because "AI adoption has grown faster than we thought it would at the beginning of the year."
	- Promised to maintain **80% gross margin**
