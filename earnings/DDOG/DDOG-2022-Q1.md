- **Source**: Datadog 2022 Q1 earnings call
- **Theme**: Moving toward developers; pricing flexibility; Azure Golden Path partnership

---

- ## Products
	- Moving more towards developers: static source code analysis, CI/CD visibility
	- Extending user experience products in APM suite to behavioral and user analytics, business analytics, and real-time BI

- ## Pricing & Competition
	- **Pricing pressure from hyperscalers**: Even with improvements from cloud providers, customers' experience is a dramatic increase in complexity. Value shifts from running infrastructure to understanding and managing it — which is what Datadog does.
	- In a position to maintain or even increase prices while delivering more value and saving customers money
	- **Pricing negotiation philosophy**: Give customers as much flexibility as possible to align what they pay with the value they get. Many tiers for storing data, just-in-time sampling, archiving, bringing back data from archives, more controls and levers.
	- 75% to 80% of business in take-or-pay subscriptions. Only ~5% pure consumption month-to-month. The rest is on-demand pricing associated with the subscription portion. Strong contractual underpinning in cases of lower consumption.

- ## Partnerships
	- **Enhanced Azure relationship**: Part of Azure's "Golden Path" for migrating into the cloud; seeing great customers onboarding through that partnership
	- Investing in GSI partnerships (Government Secure Intranet)
