- ## Financials
	- Revenue: **$469M**, +44% YoY, +8% QoQ, above high end of guidance

- ## Customers
	- ~**23,200 customers**, up from ~18,800 a year prior
	- ~**2,780 customers with ARR ≥ $100K**, up from ~2,010; these customers generated ~**85% of ARR**
	- **317 customers with ARR ≥ $1M**, up from 216
	- **37% of the Fortune 500** are Datadog customers, up from 30% a year prior; median Datadog ARR for F500 customers is in the hundreds of thousands of dollars
