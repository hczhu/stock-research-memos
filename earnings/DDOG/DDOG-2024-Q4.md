- **Source**: Datadog Q4 2024 earnings call
- **Theme**: 400+ features in 2024; 45% Fortune 500 penetration; AI integration growing; hyperscaler capacity constraints noted

---

- ## Products
	- Released **400+ new features and capabilities** in 2024, including:
		- Kubernetes autoscaling
		- Datadog monitoring for Oracle Cloud Infrastructure
		- Fully interoperable with OTel components by embedding the OTel collector directly within the Datadog agent
		- Error tracking: view and manage errors across user sessions, applications, and logs in one place
		- Mobile app testing for iOS and Android
		- **BizAI**
			- Launching BizAI for incident management
			- Previewing BizAI for autonomous investigations
	- GPU monetization not yet strong ("Not monetizing GPUs very well on our end"). Even with that caveat, Datadog still grows faster than hyperscalers.
	- **Growth opportunities in infra monitoring (ARR $1.25B)**:
		- GPU management/optimization
		- On-prem IoT fleets
		- Network monitoring
		- Still less than half of the Fortune 500 as customers

- ## Customers
	- **45% of the Fortune 500** are Datadog customers, up from 42% in 2023. Median Datadog ARR for F500 customers still less than $500K
	- **3,500 customers** used one or more Datadog AI integrations (ML, AI, LLM usage)
	- **7,000 customers** using one or more security products
	- New customer deals:
		- Landed a 7-figure annualized deal with a major U.S. financial institution struggling with high costs and expensive logging tools
		- Landed a 7-figure annualized deal with a large retail company that had built a home-grown OSS observability tool with poor visibility into customer journeys
		- Signed a 7-figure annualized expansion with a leading security software company that had built a home-grown log management tool using OSS — time-consuming to maintain, costly, and performed poorly

- ## Events
	- All three major hyperscalers missed their Q4 revenue forecasts, citing AI capacity constraints — indicating AI infrastructure demand is outpacing supply
