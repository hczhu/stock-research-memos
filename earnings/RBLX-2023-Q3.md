- ## Monetization
	- Introducing **subscriptions for developers** so their experiences can host subscriptions
	- Infrastructure-related capex projection: **<$100M in 2024** (down from $426M in 2022; $255M in 2023 YTD)
	- 30% of revenue attributable to Robux sales through Apple App Store; 17% through Google Play Store
	- Primary operating leverage focus: infrastructure and personnel costs, not payment processing fees

---

- ## Users and Engagement
	- Strong DAU growth and higher monetization growth in Germany, India, and Brazil
	- Launched Roblox on **Meta Quest** and **PlayStation**
	- Platform quality improvements in Japan drove material DAU growth there
