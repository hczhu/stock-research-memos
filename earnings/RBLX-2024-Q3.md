- **Source**: Roblox 2024 Q3 earnings call and filings
- **Theme**: Peak payers in company history; ad monetization expanding; ecosystem health metrics improving

---

- ## Directions
	- Evolve platform technology in support of game creators' ambitions and an expanded ecosystem
	- Increase developer opportunities through innovation in economic systems, including commerce integration partnership with **Shopify**
	- Amplify creators' growth through on- and off-platform organic and paid acquisition
	- Long-term target: **10% of the global gaming software market** (~$180B in content revenue; 3B+ people play games annually)

---

- ## Monetization
	- **Peak payers** in company history this quarter
	- Ads
		- Paid sponsored tiles on the homepage
		- Teleport traffic ads
		- Search ads
		- Video ads
		- Shopify partnership enables developers and brands to sell in-game
	- Focus on technologically supporting high-value game genres (e.g., Sports)

---

- ## Users and Engagement
	- Growth tactics:
		- **Search and Discovery**: AI-driven discovery improvements advanced personalization → more relevant experiences and items → higher engagement and spending
		- **Virtual Economy and Marketplace**: Dynamic price floors, UGC For All, in-experience price optimizations boosted monetization
		- **App Performance**: Improvements to launch time, crash rate reductions, frame rate increases → longer uninterrupted play times
		- **Live Ops**: Platform-wide events attracted new users and grew returning users, increasing DAUs
	- Indicators of long-term platform health:
		- Developer community publishing more significant content updates of higher quality
		- Emergence of new viral experiences breaking into top charts
		- Spending growth becoming more evenly distributed among the top 100 experiences
