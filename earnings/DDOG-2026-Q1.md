- **Source**: Datadog Q1 2026 earnings call
- **Theme**: First $1B revenue quarter; ARR >$4B; hyperscaler AI lab deals; 22 AI-native customers >$1M; FedRAMP High; ASIC monitoring

---

- ## Headline
	- **First quarter with revenue exceeding $1B**; ARR exceeded $4B
	- AI-native cohort now has **22 customers spending >$1M annually**, **5 spending >$10M annually**
	- Landed 7-figure and 8-figure annualized deals with AI research divisions of major hyperscalers (Amazon/Microsoft/Meta research labs)
	- "AI training has evolved from a niche, artisanal research activity into a critical production workload. Now scaling by orders of magnitude, it requires constant reliability — any training downtime directly benefits the competition."

- ## Products
	- **FedRAMP High** certification from the U.S. government
	- Launched next datacenter in the U.K. (a region in hyperscalers)
	- **GA'd GPU Monitoring**
	- Launched **Datadog Experiments** for A/B tests
	- GA'd **Datadog MCP Server**
	- **Security**
		- Bits AI Security Analyst GA'd
	- **ASIC monitoring**
		- "Traditional monitoring tools really fail on custom silicon and Datadog handles it well. Also new telemetry including high-bandwidth memory."

- ## Customers
	- **Business model on AI agents**: "We don't care whether most of the usage is humans or agents. Our business model lends itself to do well. We are usage-based; it doesn't really matter where the usage is coming from."
	- Landed 2 large deals — a 7-figure and an **8-figure annualized deal** with the AI research divisions of 2 major hyperscalers
		- "We started landing customers that are actually hyperscalers that have a whole host of homegrown technologies and that are using us specifically in their super intelligence labs to help monitor their workloads, accelerate the training runs, monitor the GPUs."
		- "Hyperscalers typically have a culture of building everything themselves." — makes these wins especially significant
	- **AI-native customers**
		- 22 spending >$1M annually
		- 5 spending >$10M annually
	- **20% of customers (6,500)** send data for 1+ AI integrations; represent **~80% of ARR**
	- Datadog MCP Server tool calls: **4x QoQ**
	- Bits Assistant messages: **12x QoQ**

- ## Events / Context (FundaAI preview, pre-earnings)
	- Core business held up well, but with bifurcation: AI-native cohort (including AI startups within AWS ecosystem) grew above expectations; traditional and mid-market enterprises turned more cautious as budgets were reallocated toward AI projects
	- Customer feedback on Bits AI was muted; enterprise customers concluded cost/benefit tradeoff did not yet pencil after testing; only AI-native cohort showed relatively positive uptake
	- OpenAI migration off Datadog progressing somewhat more slowly than expected; full migration now expected around 4Q26
	- **Anthropic's usage continues to grow rapidly off a low base**
