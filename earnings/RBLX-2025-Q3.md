- ## Directions
	- Targeting older users to increase revenue; developing a new category called "Novel" games with different visuals and mechanics (focus on shooters, RPGs, sports)
	- AI: Enhanced Safety, Augmented Creation, Powered Discovery
	- Apps, Social, and Discovery
		- Launched **Moments** in September as a new way for users to engage with content on Roblox
		- Creator dashboard showing how experiences perform vs. comparable experiences
	- Infrastructure
		- Cloud bursting technology launched to optimize owned infrastructure while serving peak loads from third-party cloud
		- Plan to deploy GPUs at scale in own data centers in Q4 to support AI training and inference loads
	- Advertising
		- Native Advertising: 30+ of top 100 creators by engagement using Ads Manager to grow their audience
		- Content Integrations: Universal Pictures' Jurassic World: Rebirth (~2 billion impressions over 3 weeks); non-endemic brands Sam's Club, Venmo, Snap

---

- ## Users and Engagement
	- Age-check data reveals younger user base than self-reported: through January 31, 45% of DAUs have age-checked
		- Of age-checked users: 35% U13, 38% ages 13–17, 27% O18
	- 18–34 cohort growing at over **50% YoY** — more than double the rate of U18 DAUs — and monetizes **40% higher**
	- Age-check rollout created: **mid-single-digit headwind** to engagement growth; **low-single-digit headwind** to bookings growth — lost hours largely from less engaged users
	- Record concurrent users: **45M in August** (Grow a Garden broke world record at ~22M; later Steal a Brainrot eclipsed 25M)
	- **7 experiences** have more than 10M DAUs; 5 of 7 created in the last 12 months
	- Roblox grows primarily via viral growth, complemented thoughtfully with paid user acquisition (runs ads on Meta apps); opportunity to incentivize content creators to use ad networks

	- In September, increased DexEx rate by **8.5%**
