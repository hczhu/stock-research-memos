- **Theme**: Sales overhaul introduces near-term execution risk even if the long-term product thesis remains intact

---

- ## Go-to-Market
	- The source deck emphasizes risk from a **sales-force overhaul**
	- Management downplayed the importance of the **100+ underperformers** because they represented only **4% of ACV**
	- The deck argues investors should not expect immediate sales-efficiency gains
	- New sales hires likely need at least **6 months** to reach full productivity
	- The transition could create elevated **attrition risk**

---

- ## Modeling Read-Through from the Source Deck
	- The source models only **50 bps** of sales-and-marketing leverage in CY23
	- It then assumes **1 point** of expansion in CY24, followed by **2 points** in each of the following years

---

- ## Read-Through
	- This is an execution quarter, not a thesis-break quarter
	- The key question is whether Cloudflare can build a more rigorous enterprise sales machine without damaging the culture or slowing product momentum
