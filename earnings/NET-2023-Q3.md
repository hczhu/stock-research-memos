- **Source**: Cloudflare Q3 2023 earnings materials cited in `Cloudflare $NET - Investment thesis.pdf`
- **Theme**: “Connectivity cloud” becomes the umbrella narrative, with DDoS still serving as a strong wedge into broader adoption

---

- ## Mission and Positioning
	- Cloudflare said it had realized what it is: a **connectivity cloud**
	- “Connectivity” means connecting people and things
	- “Cloud” means the batteries are included: programmable, secure, intelligent, and scalable

---

- ## Customers
	- A **Fortune 500 technology company** used Cloudflare’s connectivity cloud to collect AI/ML data from customers while maintaining high privacy
	- An **international technology company** approached Cloudflare during a large-scale **DDoS attack**
	- A **leading ad-tech company** needed a platform that could support spikes up to **3M requests per second**
	- Management noted DDoS often acts as the **“milk in the grocery store”** that opens the door to selling other products

---

- ## Read-Through
	- This quarter shows that Cloudflare’s legacy strengths in DDoS and traffic handling still matter strategically
	- Rather than being obsolete, those products can be the initial trust anchor that leads to broader platform adoption
