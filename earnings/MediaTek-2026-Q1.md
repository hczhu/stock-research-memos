- **Source**: MediaTek 2026 Q1 earnings call; CEO Rick Tsai
- **Thesis**: MediaTek is transitioning from a handset chip company into an AI infrastructure company. The ASIC business is accelerating faster than management's original roadmap.

  ---
- ## Headline Number: ASIC Revenue Guide Doubled
- Q4 2026 ASIC revenue guidance raised from **$1B → $2B** (doubled on the call)
- ASIC TAM revised upward: **$67B → $70–80B**; timeline pulled in from 2028 **to 2027**; target market share held at **15%**
- Second ASIC chip is larger with a higher ASP — likely a Google TPU order
- Incremental value captured on both **silicon and packaging**
- Management invested in **two parallel packaging architectures** — "demand for AI infrastructure is so strong, packaging is critical"

  ---
- ## ASIC Projects
- **Google (first project)**: progressing well; expected to contribute **~$2B in 2026 Q4** and **multiple billions in 2027**
- **Meta (second project)**: design underway in close collaboration with customer and supply chain partners; mass production targeted by end of 2027
- **Technologies in development**:
	- Next-generation 400G high-speed SerDes
	- 64G die-to-die interconnect
	- Advanced 3.5D platform — hybrid packaging architecture combining 2.5D (horizontal) and 3D (vertical) integration on a single substrate
	- Custom HBM solutions and integrated voltage regulators (IVR)

  ---
- ## Handset Segment
- Mobile revenue down **17% QoQ** in Q1; management guides another **~15% decline** for the full year
- Management characterizes this as a **DRAM supply constraint**, not a demand problem
- The more exciting handset narrative: **agentic AI driving edge compute demand** — phones, automotive, wearables
- Automotive compute requirement cited at **~40W** — far exceeding phone-class workloads

  ---
- ## Key Q&A Takeaways
- **On Google's in-house chip capability**: CEO Rick Tsai's response was direct — not concerned; priority is capturing share in an accelerating market first
- **On ASIC margin impact**: management stated clearly that ASIC projects are **accretive to both operating margin and EPS**

  ---
- ## Technology Milestones
- **2nm SoC**: management claims MediaTek will be the **first in the industry to tape out** a 2nm SoC
- **CPO (co-packaged optics)**: MediaTek acknowledges being a late entrant but views **2028–2029** as the real demand window and expects to be ready by then
