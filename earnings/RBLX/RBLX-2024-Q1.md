---

- ## Monetization
	- **Payer lifetime accounting change** (effective April 1, 2024): estimated average lifetime of a payer changed from 28 months to 27 months
		- Change increases 2024 revenue by ~$100M
		- Partially attributed to COVID-era payer cohorts (higher average lifetimes) dropping out of the calculation
	- Reduced Infra, Trust & Safety expenses by shifting to AI-driven moderation and optimizing infra
	- Video ads made available to all advertisers
	- Advertising partners: PubMatic, Walmart, IAS (for ad measurement)
	- **David Vespe** (20-year Google ads business veteran) hired as new head of ads engineering

---

- ## Users and Engagement
	- Unseasonal decline in engagement — broad-based across regions, ages, and platforms — in back half of April and early May
	- Recovery: DAUs, Hours, and Bookings in the US & Canada returned to around **20%+ YoY** during that period
