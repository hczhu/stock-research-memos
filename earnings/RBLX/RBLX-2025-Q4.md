- **Source**: Roblox 2025 Q4 earnings call and filings

---

- ## Directions
	- Focus on novel games for older players that monetize better: shooters, RPGs, sports, and racing
	- Expanding the definition of a game on the platform

---

- ## Users and Engagement
	- 18+ cohort grew over **50% YoY** and monetizes **40% higher** than younger users
	- Hit **45 million concurrent players**
		- "Steal of Brain Rot" peaked at 25.4M concurrent players
	- Users engage with over **24 unique experiences per month** on average in 2025

---

- ## AI
	- In-house world model for gaming; internal world model as a service usable for creation (walking/painting worlds) and potentially integrating with Roblox Corporation Moments
	- Running over **400 AI models**
	- Training NPCs with **13 billion hours** of not just video data but intrinsic 3D world data

---

- ## Monetization
	- Margin down due to increased DexEx (developer exchange) rate

---

- ## Infrastructure
	- As of December 31, 2025, Roblox Cloud uses over **150,000 servers** across **25 regional data centers** distributed across North America, South America, Asia-Pacific, and Europe

---

- ## Distribution Costs
	- Made tangible progress in Q4 2025 reducing payment processing fees by moving more business to first-party distribution channels
